Energy Modeling

Home/Services/Energy Modeling
Overview

Evaluating a building's performance before it's built, not after.

Energy modeling lets us evaluate how a building's envelope, lighting and HVAC systems will actually perform before construction, rather than finding out after the fact.

We assess equipment efficiency and lighting power density, and help projects put together the documentation needed to meet local energy-code requirements.

Where it makes sense, we also factor in control strategies such as occupancy sensors and daylighting, which reduce running costs without adding complexity for the occupants.

What We Deliver

Compliance DocumentationEnergy-code compliance documentation prepared for submission.
HVAC Energy ModelingModeling used to inform and validate system selection.
Lighting Power CalculationsLighting power density calculated against code limits.
Control Strategy PlanningIntegration of occupancy sensors, daylighting and similar controls.
