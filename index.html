Who We Are

About Mechatron Engineering

Mechatron Engineering was established in 2021 to provide construction services professionally, focusing on extending a level of competence and commitment for the satisfaction of clients in their entirety.

The company started its initial construction project in the textile industry and is currently working with major clients in the same industry for construction & MEP. Mechatron is in the process of expanding services for construction & MEP activities in other major industrial, commercial and government sectors.
